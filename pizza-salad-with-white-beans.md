## Ingredients

### **Marinated Beans**

- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 1/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 garlic clove, halved
- Kosher salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
- 8 basil sprigs, chopped OR 1 rosemary sprig left whole
- Shake of dried red pepper flakes
- 2 (15 - ounce) cans, drained rinse or 3 cups cooked cooked cranberry, cannellini, or great northern beans

### Salad

- 1 large bunch Bibb or Little Gem Lettuce, 8 Cups Torn
- 2 cups cherry or heirloom tomatoes quartered or sliced
- 2 tablespoons finely minced onion
- 1 cup mozzarella, 1/2 cubes
- 8 Large basil leaves chopped
- 1/4 cup grated parmesean
- croutons
- optional: chopped salmi or prosciutto

---

## Preparation

### Marinated Beans

1. In a medium bowl, whisk together, vinegar, olive oil, garlic, salt, black pepper, basil, red pepper flakes and beans
2. Cover and allow to marinate for at least 15 minutes, but as long as you have. Keeps in refrigerator 3 to 5 days.

### Salad

1. In a large shallow bowl, toss the lettuce, tomatoes, onion, mozzarella, basil, and parmesan.
2. Toss with beans with marinade, and croutons
