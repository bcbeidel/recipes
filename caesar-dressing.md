## Ingredients

- 1 small garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
- 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 heaping tablespoon mayo
- 3 tablespoon grated parmesan
- salt and pepper to taste

---

## Preparation

1. In a blender or food processor, puree the garlic, mustard, lemon juice, vinegar, olive oil, and mayo until smooth.
2. Add parmesan and give a few pulses just to incorporate the cheese. Season with salt and pepper
3. Store in refrigerator, covered for up to 1 week.
