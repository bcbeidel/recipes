## Ingredients

---

- Trader Joe’s Miso Ginger Broth (I ‘soup’ mine up with a few spoonfuls of miso or bouillon paste, a dash of soy sauce + black vinegar, and a drizzle of honey)
- 1 bunch of scallions
- 1 bunch of cilantro
- Trader Joe’s frozen [Chicken Cilantro Mini Wontons](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/products/pdp/chicken-cilantro-mini-wontons-099085)
- Trader Joe’s [Chili Crunch](https://www.traderjoes.com/home/products/pdp/crunchy-chili-onion-062480)

  

---

## Preparation

1. Heat broth over medium heat and add the enhancements.
2. While it’s simmering, roughly chop the spinach, cilantro and scallions.
3. Pop in your wontons, then add the chopped greens just before serving so they soften just a bit.
4. Top with chili crunch.
