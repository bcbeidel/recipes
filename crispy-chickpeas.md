## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 3 cups cooked, or 2-15 oz cans chickpeas - rinsed and drained
- kosher salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 14 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/8 teaspoon cayenne

  

---

## Preparation

- Preheat oven to 450
- Mix spices in a small bowl.
- Toss oil and chickpeas, place on a sheet pan.
- Roast for 30 minutes, or until chickpeas are crispy.
- Place the chickpeas in a paper towel lined bowel, lightly blot out excess oil.
- Remove the paper towel from the bowl, mix the chickpeas with spice mixture.
