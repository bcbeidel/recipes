## Ingredients

- 2 heads of romaine lettuce, torn or chopped into 1-peices
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, sliced
- 2 tablespoons minced red onions, or scallions (white and light green parts only)
- 3 cups [[crispy-chickpeas]]
- [[caesar-dressing]]
- 4 [[perfect-boiled-eggs]]

---

## Preparation

Combine romaine, tomatoes, red onion, chickpeas, dressing, and croutons in a giant bowl, and serve with soft boiled eggs if using, or parmesan.
