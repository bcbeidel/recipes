## Ingredients

- ½ cup whole raw almonds
- ½ cup almond flour (or raw almonds in a food processor and process ~10sec until fine meal)
- ½ cup raw walnuts or mixed nuts
- 1 ½ cup rolled oats
- ⅔ cup mixed dried fruit-apricots, cherries, cranberries etc.
- ½ cup raw pepita or sunflower seeds
- ⅓ cup shredded unsweetened coconut
- 2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- ⅓ to ½ cup maple syrup or other liquid sweetener
- 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
- 2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

---

## Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 275. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Place almond flour or processed almond in a large bowl.
3. Place 1/2 cup almonds and the walnuts/ mixed nuts to processor and process for 5 seconds-you want some larger pieces and some finer meal OR use almond flour. Add to bowl.
4. Add oats, dried fruit, seeds, and shredded coconut, stir to combine.
5. Add maple syrup, melted oil and vanilla and stir well.
6. Spread onto the sheet pan and gently press it down to compact it slightly, approx ½ inch thick.
7. Bake for 20 minutes. Rotate pan and bake 18-25 minutes-until golden brown on the bottom and firm to the touch.
8. Cool for one hour before breaking apart
